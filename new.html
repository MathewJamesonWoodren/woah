The Importance of Cats

Cats have walked among humans since the first civilizations in the fertile cresent and while time has passed their importance has not wained. Cats were first domesticated as ratters who would catch rodents and prevent them from contaminaing grain. Sea faring societies with acees to these animals quickly utilized them on their ships for similar purposes of guarding food from pests. Over the centuaries however an abundance of foklore formed around cats on ships and their supernatural abilities to detect storms. When cats borded ships, they quickly spread all over eurasia, africa, and eventually the americas being not only beloved pets and sailors but also good survivalists that quickly made any land their home. Many societies considered cats holy or supernatural some even beleived they had connections to the god such as egyptians with their diety Bastet the god of protection, pleasure, and good health.
